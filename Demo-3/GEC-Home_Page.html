Visionn

Set high academic standards in Engineering education and research and accomplish this goal

We are committed to:
  • Become a center of excellence in information technology, CAD/CAM, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering
  • Establish sustainable partnership with Industry. Offer Continuing Education Programs to fulfill the needs of all stakeholders.
  • Facilitate development of the students attitudes and behavior in addition to technical competencies.
  • Facilitate the development of entrepreneurs.Provide equal training and development opportunities for women, handicapped persons and weaker sections of society.
  • Become recognized research center.Establish strong linkage with Alumni / Industry for institutional development.
  • Modernize and enhance the institutional infrastructure.
Mission

Establish high quality engineering programmers in partnership with other eminent institutes of national importance and train students to become leaders and managers of technology with academic excellence.
